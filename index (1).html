About Velvet Curtain

Founded for the stage, not the waiting room.

Velvet Curtain Interpreting was created with a single purpose: to bring world-class ASL interpretation to live theater. Not as one offering among many, but as the entire mission.

Founded by Alicia Beinbrech, a working ASL interpreter with a deep love of theatrical performance, Velvet Curtain exists because Deaf and hard-of-hearing audiences deserve more than accurate translation — they deserve the music, the comedic timing, the heartbreak, and the spectacle, delivered with the same artistry as what's happening on stage.

Every production is treated as its own world to study, prepare for, and perform alongside.
