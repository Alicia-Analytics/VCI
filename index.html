"Where interpreters, Deaf interpreters, and the Deaf community come together to share a love of the performing arts."

Velvet Curtain Interpreting was created with a single purpose: to bring world-class ASL interpretation to live theater. Not as one service among many — as the entire mission. Then create one hub for the theater lovers in the Deaf community to find their next show to enjoy!

Our interpreters are artists. They come to every production prepared, passionate, and ready to perform alongside the cast.
